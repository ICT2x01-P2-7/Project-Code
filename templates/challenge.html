How to play

  1. Select a difficulty from the options below
  2. Create your challenge by clicking on which box you placed your obstruction on
  3. Once ready, click the ready button
  4. Allow the instructor to approve the obstacle on the real map
  5. Once approved, try to get your car to the finishing line in the least amount of tries as possible!
